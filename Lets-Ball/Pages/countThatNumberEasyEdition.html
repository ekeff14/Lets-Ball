Counting Game: First Edition

Recommendation: Nursery (ages 3-4) to Year 1 (ages 5-6)

Streak: 0

How to Play

  1. Tap the big Play button to begin your adventure.
  2. Numbers will appear on the screen in a random order.
  3. Starting with the tiniest number, touch each one in order from smallest to biggest.
  4. If you touch the wrong number, 3 seconds will be removed from your time.
  5. Keep tapping the numbers in order until they are all gone or the time reaches 0.
  6. Touch the Help button to assist you if you are stuck, but be careful you only have 5 times to use it.
  7. You have 200 seconds to sort out the numbers
  8. Try to complete the game as quickly as possible!

    9. Game over? Check how you did over here!
