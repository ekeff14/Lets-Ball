Counting Game: Second Edition

Recommendation: Suitable for 3rd to 4th grade students

Streak: 0

How to Play

  1. Tap the big Play button to begin your adventure.
  2. Numbers will appear on the screen in a random order.
  3. Starting with the tiniest number, touch each one in order from smallest to biggest, this time we have negative numbers.
  4. If you touch the wrong number, 4 seconds will be removed from your time.
  5. Keep tapping the numbers in order until they are all gone or the time reaches 0.
  6. Touch the Help button to assist you if you are stuck, but be careful you only have 5 times to use it.
  7. Try to complete the game as quickly as possible!
    8. Game over? Check how you did over here!
