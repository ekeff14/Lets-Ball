Guess That Number

Recommendation: Reception (ages 4-5) to Year 2 (ages 6-7)

Guess a number between 1 and 50:

Attempts Left: 25

Time Left: 300 seconds

How to Play:

  • Tap the Play button to begin.
  • Guess a number between 1 and 50 then type it in.
  • Hit Guess to see if you're right.
  • You get 25 tries to find the secret number.
  • If you are stuck Press Hint for a little help!
